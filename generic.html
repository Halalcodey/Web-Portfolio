The Fire-Fighting Car

For my electronics project, I developed an innovative fire-fighting car, showcasing my skills in both hardware integration and software programming. This autonomous vehicle is equipped with an Arduino Uno as its central controller, a flame sensor for detecting fires, and a servo motor to maneuver the extinguishing mechanism. The car is programmed in C++, utilizing precise algorithms to navigate towards the fire and extinguish it efficiently. Additionally, I integrated various components such as DC motors for movement, a water pump for extinguishing flames, and a power supply module for stable operation.

This project not only demonstrates my proficiency in using microcontrollers and sensors but also highlights my ability to write robust code for real-time applications. By incorporating elements of robotics and automation, the fire-fighting car exemplifies my capacity to solve practical problems through innovative engineering solutions. This project was a significant learning experience, reinforcing my understanding of embedded systems and their applications in safety and emergency response scenarios. Overall, it reflects my dedication to leveraging technology for impactful, real-world applications.
